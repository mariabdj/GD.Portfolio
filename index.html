// about me

Design is my love language 💕

I'm Maria, a CS graduate who is also a graphic designer. I mix my tech side with my creative side to make visuals people actually feel something when they look at.

I do everything from social media posts to full packaging design. I also make AI art that looks nothing like AI, realistic, clean, on-brand. I work mostly in Canva and I know how to get great results fast.

I started my own brand Rukonsky, I teach, and I work remotely for a French company (part-time). I pay for every tool I use myself, no shortcuts.
